Questions

The stuff people ask.

How many people can play?
Anywhere from 10 to 300+. Smaller teams play as individuals or pairs; bigger groups split into tables. Big groups are no trouble at all.
How long does it run?
About 90 minutes for the full night, though we flex it around your schedule. A tight after-workshop slot or a long evening both work.
Do you travel to us?
The Full Experience is hosted in person across Valencia, Madrid, Murcia and Santiago de Compostela. A self-run Express version, for everywhere else, is coming soon.
Our office, or a venue?
Either. We'll run it in your space with our AV, or recommend and coordinate a venue that fits your group and budget.
English or Spanish?
Both, and we mix them for international teams. Tell us your room and we'll pitch it right.
How far ahead should we book?
Two weeks is comfortable for a fully custom hosted night. Tighter than that? Ask anyway, we'll always try to make it work.
