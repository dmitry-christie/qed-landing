Who we are

Started by three friends who built a better quiz night.

Dmitry, Stu and Evgeny started Quiz Eat Drink in Valencia to build something more than a pub quiz: a room full of strangers who leave as friends, laughing over rounds they're still arguing about the next morning.

In March 2025 they partnered with Matt to launch Tardeos de Trivia, bringing that same idea to Spain and tying it to something people here already do well: long lunches, good food, and friends and family already gathered round the table. Come pull up a chair, whether you're there to win or just to argue about the answers.
