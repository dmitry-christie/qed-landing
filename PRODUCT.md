# Product

## Register

product

## Users

English-speaking expats, local residents, and tourists in Valencia who want social nights out. They discover QED through Instagram, word of mouth, or Google. Typically in groups of 2–5, looking for something fun on a weeknight. Context: casual, often on mobile, sometimes already planning over WhatsApp with friends before they even open the app.

## Product Purpose

Book spots at pub quiz nights across Valencia venues. Find which venue is running a quiz this week, pick one, reserve the spot. The reservation is frictionless (email + name + group size), and the design should convey the energy of the event so strongly it closes the conversion without hard sell.

## Brand Personality

Warm, irreverent, confident. Like a good pub quiz host: knowledgeable but not stuffy, slightly cheeky, genuinely fun. Three words: warm, sharp, alive.

## Anti-references

Generic event-booking apps (Eventbrite, Meetup): too corporate, too neutral. Anything that looks like a SaaS template applied to events. The design should feel handmade and local, not generated. Identical-card grids, icon-in-box rows, and symmetrical 2x2 feature lists all read as AI output and undermine the brand.

## Design Principles

1. Energy before information: the vibe must land before the facts are read
2. Handmade over systematic: variation, rhythm, and character beat uniform grids
3. Honest fun: irreverent copy and warm visuals, never desperate or hard-selling
4. Frictionless reservation: no steps users don't need, no forms they can't complete in 30 seconds
5. Local character: feels specific to Valencia and QED, not a generic event template

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. aria-labels present on icon buttons. Functional in bright outdoor light on mobile. English and Spanish content (some events in Spanish).
