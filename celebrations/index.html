Questions

The stuff people ask.

How personalised does it get?
Very. We build a whole round (or more) from the details and photos you send about the guest of honour or the couple, then balance it with general questions so everyone can join in.
How many guests can play?
From an intimate 2-table dinner up to a 200-guest party. Small groups play in pairs, big ones split into teams with a live leaderboard.
Do you travel to us?
The hosted Full Experience runs in person across Valencia, Madrid, Murcia and Santiago de Compostela. A self-run Express version, for everywhere else, is coming soon.
Is it family-friendly?
It can be. Tell us the crowd and we'll pitch it accordingly, all-ages for a family birthday, or a little spicier for a stag-adjacent night.
English or Spanish?
Both, and we mix them for international guest lists. Perfect for a wedding with two sides who don't share a first language.
How far ahead should we book?
Two weeks gives us time to write a properly personal quiz. Closer to the date? Ask anyway, we'll always try to make it work.
